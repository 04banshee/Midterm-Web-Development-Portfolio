David Wiles hails from California. Born and raised in a small town named Oroville . He graduated high school and tried his hand in the construction trade for a few years. Joining the military at age 25 was a challenge. In the military he saw the world and made a better life for himself. David excelled in the military and retired with an honorable discharge. The military has many discharges, and receiving an honorable discharge is what all service members strive to attain. After 19 years of service in the USMC David retired Honorably .

After the military David relocated to the Muscle Shoals/TriCities area with his wife Christy . The experience and skills David brought from the military did not transition to the local area. David contacted the Veterans Administration (VA) for assistance for locating meaningful career opportunities. The VA and David devised a Vocational Rehabilitation plan that included finishing his education. Under the plan, David is returning to school and finishing a degree in Information Technology (IT) he started while on active duty.
